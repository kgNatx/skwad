## Feature Highlights

**Post 3 — Spectrum View**
Skwad shows you a live spectrum view of every pilot's channel in your session. See overlaps before they ruin your flight. It's like a spotter for your video feed.

**Post 4 — Smart Channel Assignment**
Not sure what channel to pick? Skwad's optimizer finds the cleanest frequency for your setup automatically. Just tell it your video system and let it do the math.

**Post 5 — QR Code Join**
Set up a Skwad session and share the QR code at the field. Everyone scans, picks their gear, and they're in. No accounts, no downloads, no hassle.
